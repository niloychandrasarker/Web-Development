Bangladesh

Bangladesh, officially known as the People's Republic of Bangladesh, is a country located in South Asia. It is bordered by India to the west, north, and east, and by Myanmar to the southeast. The Bay of Bengal lies to the south of Bangladesh. With a population of over 160 million people, Bangladesh is the eighth-most populous country in the world.

Bangladesh gained its independence from Pakistan in 1971, following a bloody liberation war. Dhaka, the capital and largest city, is a vibrant and bustling metropolis. The country's official language is Bengali, and the majority of its population practices Islam.

Bangladesh is known for its lush green landscape, extensive river systems, and the fertile Bengal Delta. Agriculture is a significant sector of the economy, with rice being the staple crop. The country is also a major exporter of ready-made garments and textile products.
